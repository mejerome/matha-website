About Our Team

Matha capital is a pan-African financial advisory firm providing leading market advice and financing solutions to Sovereigns, Corporates and Institutional names across Africa. The firm was founded in 2017 by a team of experienced top international bankers, bringing together more than a combined 80 years of experience and expertise in specialized areas of finance. Matha Capital continues to grow to cover an increasing amount of strategic transactions in an even wider geographic footprint on the sub-continent.

With offices in Abidjan and now Kigali, we arrange and structure financing for a variety of strategic transactions for clients operating in diversified sectors of the economy. Matha Capital's vision is to be the best innovative financial solutions firm to sovereigns corporate and institutions on the continent, managed by seasoned African financiers bringing their wealth of experience to African businesses and public sector entities.
